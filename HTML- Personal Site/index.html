I am a professor at Cornell University. My research focuses on the interaction of algorithms and networks, the roles they play in large-scale social and information systems, and their broader societal implications. I am a member of the National Academy of Sciences, the National Academy of Engineering, and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.
